Nova themes

  • professional
  • wordpress
  • frontend
  • backend

blugiant.com/nova | December 2014–Ongoing

Nova was created as an alternative solution to Blu Giant's custom built websites. Nova themes are premade and come with the ability to edit colors, the navigation bar and footer, the logo, pages, and sections on pages. All the Nova themes come with a page builder that I coded myself from the ground up, which allows clients to easily add and edit content, backgrounds, icons, buttons, and more.

The Nova themes have honestly become my pet project. I enjoy the challenge of creating themes that have just enough customization to allow clients to insert their brands as well as easily be able to edit content, colors, and imagery.

ervinandsmith.com | July 2014

During my internship at Ervin & Smith, my fellow intern and I created the Ervin & Smith WordPress theme from scratch. This was one of the first WordPress themes I created. I primarily coded the front-end and became fluent in SASS, while the other intern did most of the back-end.

While I did not code the majority of the back-end, I did code a significant portion for Ervin & Smith's feed at the bottom of the home page which intermingles blog posts, Facebook posts, and tweets. This required integration with Facebook and Twitter APIs.
